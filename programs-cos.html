College of Science & General Studies · Programs

The scientific foundation every specialist needs.

01

B.Sc. in Life Sciences

A foundation in biology, chemistry and life sciences research methods, preparing students for graduate study, healthcare careers or biotech and research roles.

02

University Preparatory Program (UPP)

An intensive foundation year in English, mathematics and study skills that prepares incoming students for the academic demands of their chosen college.

Why Science & General Studies

The starting point for every discipline

  • ✓ Rigorous math, physics & chemistry foundations
  • ✓ Small classes with hands-on lab work
  • ✓ A natural bridge into Medicine, Pharmacy & Engineering
  • ✓ UPP support for students needing an academic foundation year
