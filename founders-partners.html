Our Heritage

Founders & Partners

Founding Vision

Built under royal patronage, grown through partnership

Alfaisal University was established in 2002 by the King Faisal Foundation, under the patronage of the family of the late King Faisal bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, with the support of prominent Saudi corporate founders. In the years since, that founding vision has been sustained by strategic partnerships with leading healthcare, industry and technology organizations that connect classroom learning to real-world practice.

Founders & Strategic Partners

The organizations behind Alfaisal

01

King Faisal Foundation

Founding Patron

Established Alfaisal University in 2002 under royal patronage as part of its broader mission to advance education, research and culture in the Kingdom.

02

Dallah Albaraka

Founding Partner

A leading Saudi conglomerate among the university's founding corporate patrons, supporting its establishment and endowment.

03

Aljomaih Automotive

Founding Partner

A longstanding Saudi enterprise among Alfaisal's founding corporate contributors, reflecting the private-sector commitment behind the university's creation.

04

Saudi Oger

Founding Partner

Contributed to Alfaisal's founding endowment and early campus development as part of the university's original group of corporate patrons.

05

Saudi Binladin Group

Founding Partner

Supported the construction and development of the Alfaisal campus as one of the university's founding corporate partners.

06

King Faisal Specialist Hospital & Research Centre

Academic & Clinical Partner

Partners with the College of Medicine on clinical training, teaching hospital placements and research collaboration for the MBBS program.

07

United Technologies

Industry Partner

Engages with the College of Engineering & Advanced Computing on applied research collaboration and student development.

08

Thales Group

Industry & Research Partner

Collaborates on engineering and technology initiatives, connecting Alfaisal students and faculty to global industry practice.

09

BAE Systems Saudi Arabia

Industry & Recruitment Partner

Engages with engineering and business students through internships, sponsorships and recruitment opportunities.

10

Boeing

Industry & Research Partner

A longstanding strategic partner supporting engineering programs, research initiatives and graduate recruitment at Alfaisal.
