A doctorate built for modern pharmacy practice.

Pharm.D.

Doctor of Pharmacy

The Pharm.D. program combines pharmaceutical sciences, clinical pharmacy and hands-on lab work, preparing graduates for the full range of modern pharmacy practice — from community pharmacy to clinical and research roles.

  • ✓ Pharmaceutical sciences & drug development fundamentals
  • ✓ Clinical pharmacy rotations across the Kingdom's healthcare sector
  • ✓ Modern lab facilities and research partnerships
  • ✓ Pathways into hospital, community and industry pharmacy
