Est. 2002

A legacy built on royal vision, sustained by academic rigor.

Alfaisal University rose on historic royal grounds in Riyadh under the patronage of the family of the late King Faisal. Today it stands among the Kingdom's most respected non-profit research universities — NCAAA-accredited and aligned with the ambitions of Saudi Vision 2030.

  • ✓ NCAAA-accredited programs across six colleges
  • ✓ Strategic partners including Boeing, Shell & King Faisal Specialist Hospital
  • ✓ Research faculty ranked among the world's top 2% of scientists
  • ✓ A living campus of 54+ nationalities in the heart of Riyadh
