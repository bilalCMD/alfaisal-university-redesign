Our Faculty

Internationally acclaimed. Personally invested.

Alfaisal University faculty are internationally acclaimed researchers and scholars. They have published extensively in their research areas and in the most reputable professional journals.

Because the majority of our classes average only 18 students, professors know you by name and have time to mentor and provide one-on-one guidance. Faculty can help connect you with research opportunities, internships, and jobs that are a good fit for you.
