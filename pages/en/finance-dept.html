What We Do

Every riyal, accounted for

The Finance & Accounting Department manages and controls the financial operations of Alfaisal University, including accounts receivable and payable, financial planning, budgeting and control, payroll, investment and treasury, and fixed asset management.

As a non-profit institution, disciplined financial management is what lets Alfaisal reinvest in scholarships, faculty and campus growth year after year.
