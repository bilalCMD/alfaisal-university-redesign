What We Do

Support for every device, every day

IT Services (ITS) provides technical support and advice on university IT services and resources to students, faculty and staff, collaborating with the campus community using innovative technologies to meet the university's strategic goals.

From classroom AV systems to the network behind every online exam, ITS keeps the technology that modern learning depends on running quietly in the background.
