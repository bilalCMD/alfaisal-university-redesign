Affiliations

Partnerships that connect Alfaisal to the world.

Our Approach

Building bridges beyond Riyadh

Since its founding, Alfaisal University has built its academic programs alongside leading international institutions — inviting distinguished universities, hospitals and research organizations to help design curricula, train faculty, and open doors for students long before graduation.

Why It Matters

What global affiliations bring to Alfaisal

  • ✓ Curricula co-designed with distinguished international academic partners
  • ✓ Faculty exchange, training and joint research collaboration
  • ✓ Shared laboratories, facilities and clinical training sites
  • ✓ Pathways into joint research for students and faculty alike
