MBBS

Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery

Delivered in partnership with King Faisal Specialist Hospital & Research Centre, the MBBS combines rigorous basic and clinical sciences with early, sustained clinical exposure — not just a final-year rotation.

  • ✓ Early clinical exposure alongside coursework

  • ✓ Small cohorts with direct faculty mentorship
