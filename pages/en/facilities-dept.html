What We Do

Campus planning, built for the long term

The Facilities Department develops an ongoing renewal plan for campus facilities and residence infrastructure through an integrated program of campus and capital planning — covering new construction, maintenance, renovations, alterations and facility renewal projects.

Beyond buildings, the department also manages and monitors procurement, food services, and security and safety across campus — the everyday systems that let 7,000+ students, faculty and staff move through campus without thinking twice about it.
