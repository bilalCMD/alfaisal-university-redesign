Founders & Partners

Founding Vision

Built under royal patronage, grown through partnership

Alfaisal University was established in 2002 by the King Faisal Foundation, under the patronage of the family of the late King Faisal bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, with the support of prominent Saudi corporate founders. In the years since, that founding vision has been sustained by strategic partnerships with leading healthcare, industry and technology organizations that connect classroom learning to real-world practice.

Founders & Strategic Partners

The organizations behind Alfaisal

King Faisal Foundation

Founding Patron

Established Alfaisal University in 2002 under royal patronage as part of its broader mission to advance education, research and culture in the Kingdom.

02

Dallah Albaraka

Founding Partner

A leading Saudi conglomerate among the university's founding corporate patrons, supporting its establishment and endowment.

03

Aljomaih Automotive

Founding Partner

A longstanding Saudi enterprise among Alfaisal's founding corporate contributors, reflecting the private-sector commitment behind the university's creation.

04

Saudi Oger

Founding Partner

05

Saudi Binladin Group

Founding Partner

BAE Systems Saudi Arabia
