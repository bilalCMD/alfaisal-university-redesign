Equality, Diversity & Inclusion

What We Do

The Equality, Diversity & Inclusion (EDI) Committee promotes diversity, equity and inclusion at Alfaisal University, ensuring everyone within the institution is included, respected and supported. The committee includes representatives from the University Council, Student Affairs, Research & Graduate Studies and Human Resources, and reports directly to the University's President.
