What We Do

A committee that answers to the President

The Equality, Diversity & Inclusion (EDI) Committee promotes diversity, equity and inclusion at Alfaisal University, ensuring everyone within the institution is included, respected and supported.

The committee may consult other staff or faculty on specific matters, and can establish sub-committees to consider particular cases — keeping EDI a living practice, not a one-time policy.
