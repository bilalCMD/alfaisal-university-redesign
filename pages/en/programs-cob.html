College of Business · Programs

Business programs built for the real world.

The College of Business offers a BBA with five areas of concentration, taught by faculty with both academic and industry experience, alongside globally recognized professional certifications.

01

BBA — Finance

Corporate finance, investment analysis and financial markets, preparing graduates for banking, asset management and corporate finance roles.

02

BBA — Accounting

Financial and managerial accounting, auditing and taxation — a strong foundation for careers in public accounting and corporate finance.

03

BBA — Management

Organizational behavior, strategy and leadership, built for students aiming toward general management and entrepreneurship.

04

BBA — Marketing

Consumer behavior, digital marketing and brand strategy, for students pursuing careers across marketing, sales and communications.

05

BBA — Operations & Project Management

Supply chain, logistics and project management principles for students headed toward operations-focused careers.

06

Executive Certifications

Exam preparation tracks for CFA, CMA, PMP, CAPM and CBDDAP — globally recognized credentials alongside the degree.
