FAQ

Frequently asked questions

When do undergraduate applications open?

+

Admission to undergraduate programs for the Fall/First Semester 2026/2027 opens March 1. We recommend applying early, as some programs have limited seats.

Is financial aid available for international students?

+

Yes. International applicants are eligible to apply for merit-based scholarships alongside Saudi and GCC students, evaluated on academic performance.

Do all programs require an entrance exam?

+

Some programs — including Medicine, Pharmacy and Engineering — require a program-specific assessment or interview in addition to academic records.

Can I apply to more than one college?

+

Yes, applicants may indicate a first and second choice of program during the online application process.

Where can I find the student handbook?

+

The Student Handbook and full policy documentation are available through the Deanship of Student Affairs, Admissions & Registration.
