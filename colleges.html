Our Colleges

Six colleges. One vision.

01

College of Business

BBA concentrations in Finance, Accounting, Management, Marketing and Operations & Project Management, plus executive certifications including CFA, CMA and PMP.

  • ✓ BBA — Finance
  • ✓ BBA — Accounting
  • ✓ BBA — Management
  • ✓ BBA — Marketing
  • ✓ BBA — Operations & Project Management
Explore Programs → Visit College of Business
02

College of Engineering & Advanced Computing

Architectural, Electrical, Industrial, Mechanical, Biomedical & Software Engineering, alongside BS programs in Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity and Data Science & Engineering.

  • ✓ Bachelor of Architectural Engineering & Architecture
  • ✓ Bachelor of Electrical, Industrial & Mechanical Engineering
  • ✓ Bachelor of Software & Biomedical Engineering
  • ✓ BS in Artificial Intelligence
  • ✓ BS in Cybersecurity & Data Science and Engineering
Explore Programs → Visit College of Engineering
03

College of Medicine

A Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) program delivered in partnership with King Faisal Specialist Hospital & Research Centre, combining rigorous science with early clinical exposure.

  • ✓ Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)
  • ✓ Early clinical exposure from year one
  • ✓ Clinical training with King Faisal Specialist Hospital
Explore Programs → Visit College of Medicine
04

College of Pharmacy

A Pharm.D. (Doctor of Pharmacy) program built around modern labs, clinical rotations and research partnerships across the Kingdom's healthcare sector.

  • ✓ Pharm.D. (Doctor of Pharmacy)
  • ✓ Modern labs & clinical rotations
  • ✓ Research partnerships across the Kingdom's healthcare sector
Explore Programs → Visit College of Pharmacy
05

College of Science & General Studies

A B.Sc. in Life Sciences and the University Preparatory Program (UPP), building the scientific and academic foundation students need before specializing.

  • ✓ B.Sc. in Life Sciences
  • ✓ University Preparatory Program (UPP)
  • ✓ Foundational math, physics & chemistry coursework
Explore Programs → Visit College of Science
06

College of Law & International Relations

An LLB program alongside specializations in Diplomacy & International Relations and Governance & Public Policy — preparing graduates for law, government and global affairs.

  • ✓ LLB (Bachelor of Laws)
  • ✓ Diplomacy & International Relations
  • ✓ Governance & Public Policy
Explore Programs → Visit College of Law
